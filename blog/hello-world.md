# Hello World

This is the first post on my personal blog. I'll use this space to share thoughts on things I'm building, reading, or thinking about — no particular cadence, just whenever something is worth writing down.

## What to expect

Mostly technical writing: projects I'm working on, problems I ran into and how I solved them, and the occasional rabbit hole. Sometimes non-technical: books, ideas, things that stuck with me.

## How this works

Posts are written in Markdown and live in the `blog/` folder of this repo. Running `node scripts/update.js` regenerates the index and makes everything show up here. Simple, no CMS required.
